# Content

Content pipeline and outputs. Blog posts, essays, and published content derived from the second brain.

## Purpose

- Publish polished content based on wiki and journal insights
- Writing and editing workspace
- Final drafts for distribution
- Content calendar and planning

## Organization

- **drafts/** — Work in progress
- **published/** — Finalized and published pieces
- **index.md** — Catalog of all content pieces

Organize by date or topic: `DD-MM-YYYY_[title-slug].md`

Examples:
- `2026-06-12_knowledge-systems.md` (draft)
- `published/2026-05-15_building-your-second-brain.md`

## Workflow

1. **Research** — Pull from wiki concepts and journal reflections
2. **Draft** — Write in drafts/
3. **Edit** — Refine and polish
4. **Publish** — Move to published/ with distribution links
5. **Track** — Update index.md with publication info
