# Concepts

Timeless topic and concept pages. Reusable knowledge that doesn't have an expiration date.

## What Goes Here

- Frameworks and mental models
- Definitions and explanations of key concepts
- Principles and patterns
- Domain knowledge and best practices
- Interconnected ideas and relationships

## File Naming

Use kebab-case for clarity: `concept-name.md`

Examples:
- `knowledge-management.md`
- `system-design-principles.md`
- `learning-strategies.md`

## Structure

Start with a brief intro, then:
- Key ideas or breakdown
- Related concepts (wikilinks `[[related-concept]]`)
- Examples where helpful
- Sources/references to raw/ material

## Relationships

Use `[[wikilink-format]]` to link to other concept pages, projects, or people.

Example: "This concept is related to [[project-management]] and influenced by [[person-name]]'s work."
